What is a Graduate Gemologist?

GIA, or the Gemological Institute of America, is the training authority in the American jewelry industry. To earn a Graduate Gemologist degree from the GIA, you must invest a substantial amount of time and money to complete and pass five course studies and three lab classes, and then pass a comprehensive final exam. Following are the 8 requirements: Diamond Essentials, Diamonds and Diamond Grading, Colored Stone Essentials, Colored Stones, Gem Identification, and Lab Classes: Diamond Grading Lab, Colored Stone Grading Lab, Gem Identification Lab. The most prestigious credential in the industry, the GIA Graduate Gemologist program gives a person the compregensive knowledge of diamonds and colored stones one needs to succeed anywhere in the industry.
